info_queue:
    used to show the 'tooltips' when hovering next to a card (steel/glass/negative description etc.)

'mod_conv' will force a change on a card(s) when specified in the 'config' field
    for example, config = { mod_conv = 'm_steel', max_highlighted = 1 } will allow 1 card to become steel
